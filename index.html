RELEVANT IMPLICATIONS

What are relevant implications?

Relevant implications are the significance or effect related to a porject, descision, or development. they highlight the why and how of an outcome.These implications guid development and must be addresed to ensure the project is a success, the project is safe and to chech the compliance of the project.

There are 13 main relevant implications for you to focus on. these 10 include:

  • Social
  • Legal
  • Intelectual Property
  • Privacy
  • Cultural
  • Ethical
  • Functionality
  • Usablility
  • Aesthetic
  • Accessibility
  • Health and Safety
  • Sustainability and Future Proofing
  • End-User Considerations

For easier understanding the 13 implications can be further split into 2 groups, one that focuses on the user and the other that focuses on the development.

Why do we use relevant implications?

we use relevant implications in technology because every piece of technology we create affects people and the world. heres this idea put simply:

to make technology safe and appropriate:

if youre designing a website or app, you need to think abut things like:

  • will i protect users personal information?
  • is it safe for different age groups.
thats part of ethical and legal implications.

to make it usable for anyone:

technology should be easy to use and accessible for al types of people:

  • can people with disabilities use it?
  • does it work on phones and different screen sizes?
this linls to usability and Accessibility implications.

to avoid harm or negative impacts:

some tech can cause problens if not thought through:

  • spreading misinformation.
  • encouraging unhealthy habits
  • excluding certain groups
so we consider social implications.

to meet user needs properly:

theres no point making something that doesnt actually help people:

  • does it solve the problem it is meant to?
  • is it effiicient and reliable?
thats part of functional implications.

to follow rules and standards.

there are laws and guidlines around technology:

  • copyright (using images,music,etc.)
  • data protection.
this is about legal implications.

in conclusion we use relevant implications to make sure technology is responsible,useful,safe,and fair, not just "working"

Why are relevalant implications important?

relevant implications are important because they make sure technology is not just functional, but responsible and effective in the real world.

if you ignore implications when designing a web page, your technology might still "work," but it could cause problems. for example, a website might not load and the content may not be accurate.

thinking about relevant implications helps you:
make better decisions.

ou consider the consequences of what you design, so you dont create something harmful or unfair.

protect users.

it ensures peoples privacy, safety, and rights are respected.

improves quality

technology becomes easier to use, more reliable, and more suitable for different users.

in conclusion, relevant implications are important because they ensure your web page is user friendly, meets the users needs, is factual and safe for all users.
